What You'll Learn

No coding required. Just curiosity and creativity.

Global Health Challenges

Explore real-world health inequities and the social determinants that shape them. Learn how AI might help expand access to care in low-resource settings around the world.

AI Without Code

Develop a working knowledge of how large language models function. Use ChatGPT, Claude, and other tools to build and test ideas without deep technical knowledge.

Design Thinking

Use design thinking to identify needs and prototype potential solutions. Practice interdisciplinary collaboration and creative problem-solving through hands-on studios.

Ethics First

Critically examine bias, privacy, equity, and accountability in AI applications. Consider who is affected by the solutions you propose, and who might be left out.

Course Philosophy

This seminar explores the transformative potential‚Äîand ethical complexity‚Äîof artificial intelligence in global health. From diagnosing diseases to expanding access to care in low-resource settings, AI is already reshaping health systems worldwide. But these tools also raise urgent questions about bias, privacy, equity, and accountability.

You'll prototype your own AI-assisted solutions through team-based design work. The course emphasizes creativity, collaboration, and ethical problem-solving. No prior experience in computer science or global health needed!
