AI in Global Health

Smart Solutions for a Healthier World

Explore the transformative potential of artificial intelligence in global health through hands-on design, ethical thinking, and collaborative innovation.

Learn More View Schedule

Course Overview

🌍

Global Health Focus

Dive into real-world health challenges and explore how AI can promote equity and expand access to care in low-resource settings.

🤖

AI Without Code

No programming experience needed! Learn to use tools like ChatGPT and Claude to prototype solutions and understand AI's capabilities.

🎨

Design Thinking

Use creative problem-solving to identify needs and prototype AI-enabled solutions through team-based design studios.

⚖️

Ethics First

Critically examine bias, privacy, equity, and accountability in AI applications for global health contexts.

Assignments & Grading

This is a low-stakes, high-curiosity course focused on engagement and creativity.

Weekly Reflections

Short responses (~250 words) connecting theory to design work

20% of grade

AI Tool Exercises

Guided explorations of AI functionality and limitations

20% of grade

Team Check-ins

Self and peer assessments, collaboration updates

15% of grade

Design Brief

Problem framing, needs analysis, ethical stakes

10% of grade

Final Project

Prototype, pitch, and rationale presented at showcase

30% of grade

Participation

Contributions to discussions and studio sessions

5% of grade
