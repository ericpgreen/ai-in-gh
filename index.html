What You'll Learn

No coding required. Just curiosity and creativity.

🌍

Global Health Challenges

Explore real-world health inequities and how AI might help expand access to care in low-resource settings around the world.

🤖

AI Without Code

Learn to use ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI tools to prototype solutions. Understand what AI can (and can't) do for health.

✏️

Design Thinking

Use human-centered design methods to identify needs and create AI-enabled solutions through hands-on studio sessions.

⚖️

Ethics First

Critically examine bias, privacy, and equity issues. Learn to build AI solutions that promote justice, not harm.
